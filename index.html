Complex Form Data Parser & CRM Integration

Plumbing & Heating Industry • September 2025

🎯 The Challenge

GB Plumbing used an infographic-style Formidable Form on WordPress where data came through webhooks split between values and image codes. The client needed complex address data (received as single-line strings) to be parsed into separate fields (street address, city, postal code) for proper CRM entry. Additionally, the form included conditional questions and special requirements that needed intelligent filtering before entering GoHighLevel CRM.

💡 The Solution

Built a sophisticated Make.com automation using Claude.ai for JSON parsing logic. Implemented Text Parser modules to intelligently split address data into component fields (street, city, postal code) and separate combined name fields into first and last names. Used Router modules with conditional filtering to process client-specific requirements and answers, ensuring only relevant data flows to GoHighLevel. The workflow automatically creates properly formatted contacts in CRM and sends instant notifications to the company with lead details and source attribution.

WooCommerce E-Store Integration with CRM

E-Commerce • September 2025

🎯 The Challenge

Grassed Artificial, an e-commerce business, needed their WordPress WooCommerce shop integrated with GoHighLevel CRM. Every time a customer requested a free sample or made a purchase online, the data needed to flow automatically into the CRM with complete order details (products, descriptions, values) and trigger notifications to the business owner. Orders required manual approval in their payment system, so only "processing" status orders needed to be captured.

💡 The Solution

Built a Make.com automation that monitors WooCommerce for new orders with "Processing" status, sorted by date created. When a new order is detected, the workflow creates a customer contact in GoHighLevel with a custom field capturing the shopping cart value. This contact is then used to automatically generate an opportunity in the "Customers Pipeline - Website Shop" where the opportunity value is extracted from the custom field. The system ensures all product information, descriptions, and order details flow seamlessly into the CRM while notifying the client of each new order.

HubSpot Internal Operational Workflow Automation

Renewable Energy - Solar & Battery Storage • 2024

🎯 The Challenge

Ceiba Renewables' solar consultants spent countless hours manually chasing clients for property details and G99/G98 documentation (UK technical requirements for generators connecting to the electricity network). Operations managers manually created Microsoft Teams channels for each won deal, tracked pipeline stages, monitored client suspensions due to unforeseen circumstances, and created reminders for SIMPRO project reviews and quotes. The manual workflow created bottlenecks, missed follow-ups, and prevented strategic planning time.

💡 The Solution

Designed and implemented a comprehensive HubSpot workflow automating the entire operational pipeline. The system automatically sends templated emails to new leads explaining G99/G98 requirements and data collection instructions. It creates internal notifications tracking lead progress through the operational pipeline, automatically provisions Microsoft Teams channels in designated sections for each project, and generates SIMPRO-related tasks for Operations Managers and Solar Consultants with built-in reminders. The workflow incorporates intelligent branching logic: if the Solar Consultant completes the custom G99/G98 field, it triggers the appropriate documentation email template to customers and creates chase tasks with reminders; if documentation is marked as N/A, the workflow bypasses this stage and advances directly to Site Survey. Throughout the process, the system automatically updates pipeline stages and monitors for client suspension flags.
